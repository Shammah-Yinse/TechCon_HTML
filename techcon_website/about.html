The History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the goal of bringing together tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated conferences in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to foster collaboration, innovation, and learning within the tech community. Our mission is to create a platform where the brightest minds in technology can share knowledge, showcase new ideas, and inspire future generations.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, has spoken at TechCon about the future of space exploration and sustainable energy. His innovative work has revolutionized the tech and automotive industries.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, delivered an insightful keynote on the impact of cloud computing and artificial intelligence on the future of business and technology.

Marissa Mayer

Marissa Mayer, former Yahoo CEO and technology executive, shared her experience on innovation and leadership in tech, inspiring thousands of aspiring women in the industry.
